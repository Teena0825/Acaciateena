Hello, I am Teena Gunpe

I born in Hyderabad, India. I began experimenting with designing and making own clothing. After graduating with a degree in art education, I began an apprenticeship with a milliner. When I finally opened own boutique in Kings Road in Hitech city, the shop became an immediate success. The colorful shift dresses with contrasting collars, and plastic trench coats, I created helped popularize a style of dressing that came to be known as the Mod Look.Hence I have started an academy for fashion designing please contact if you need more information and join us.
